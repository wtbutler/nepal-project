Exploring Tibet

The landscape of a country or region is massively influential in determining how people move throughout that region. The same is true for stories and religious myths. In Tibet, the geography ranges from mildly inconvenient to nearly insurmountable. This changes how stories are spread by encouraging insularity in communities, especially in the mountainous regions. This website provides an exploration of how stories have changed or stayed the same in various regions of Tibet. With a visual aid, it becomes easier to see the effects of difficult terrain on the stories told there.
